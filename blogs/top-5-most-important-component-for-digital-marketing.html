1. SEARCH ENGINE OPTIMIZATION

When it comes to digital marketing SEO plays a very important role. This is because you might have a very appealing website that features all your customer needs; however, it might not ever get chances it visits it if its lack of search engines optimization features. Now you might be wondering how it works and helps your business work. Search engine optimization makes your website to rank at top and help social engines like Google, Bing, Yahoo and others to find your website easily.

A study shows that more than 75% of people do like to visit second page of their search result. That means the websites at the top list of search engines at first page (SERPs) generates more traffic and eventually leads also. Therefore, the goal of any business using digital marketing platforms is to get in first page of the search result in any search engine (most of the case Google) to get more traffic and leads. And that is where SEO strategy helps you to rank at top.

#Here are few strategies that helps you to boost your search engine ranking

  • Create contents that reflects your business/brands or product/services. Additionally, the contents must match with what your targeted audience are looking for otherwise it doesn’t make sense.
  • Uses of keywords also plays important role to get the best search result outcomes.
  • Update your website page more often.
  • Use link from other websites.
  • Uses of meta description tags.

2. PAY PER CLICK (PPC) ADVERTISING

There are few price/cost model options available to the business to create add. Among them Cost per click and cost per impression are the most popular and cost effective costing method that helps to generates more leads for your business. Cost per impression methods may potentially help to reach more customers however, pay per click helps meanwhile leads to reach the customer that actually click the link (visit website).

So, we only pay per click using Pay per click module meanwhile, Cost per impression is a cost per reaching a customer’s not necessary to click the link (ads). So, most expert for recommended Pay per click as the ideal way to reach targeted customer and generate more leads. If you have advance options available which allows you to choose specific types of audience/target customer (segmentation) to reach exact potential customer and save your ads cost. But for this you must have solid understating how this ads module works and your targeted audience.
