#2 Start your own Drop-shipping business.

This is an ideal e-commerce business if you have some idea/experience running an online store or even if you have an overview of how retails business operates. Even with no experience it is one of the top success e-commerce business modules. You need little amount of finance to start your drop-shopping business . It is a virtual store you don’t need to buy the stock and store them, you can create your design, and store those items on your online store, all you need to focus on marketing. Identify the niche/target market and secure a suppliers (domestic/local), build your own website/store, create/promote your products through various ads like Facebook ads and sell it. If you are beginner and want to start drop shopping business you can visit shopify to create your own store. Teespring is my favorite e-commerce platform to design and sell product online.

Estimated average earning per months is $1000- $5000 per month with diversify products.
