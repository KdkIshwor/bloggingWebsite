Start with Inner Engineering

I found inner engineering book in November 2019 and brought from the Amazon kindle to read it. First week into the reading i found most of my inner questions being answered in an simplest way. What could have taken me years to understand came down to me in an instant experientially and i was more awake than ever. Moreover i was consious than ever before and 100% involve in all what i am doing. I taught myself coding and making websites, and now writing articles following the principles of inner engineering. I downloaded the audio version of book and since then it has been my constant companion. So, today i decided to bring that note of mine into the small article to summarise the book knowledge for everyone to read.

Part One contains five chapters: When I Lost My Sense, The Way Out Is In, Design Your Destiny, No Boundary No Burden, ...And Now, Yoga. Part Two has four chapters: Body, Mind, Energy, and Joy.

Book starts with the description of author, his path of enlightement through yoga and stories of Akbar and Birbal and many more. The book is full with small stories of shankaran Pilla(a gentleman from India) and many yogic stories and their achievements of different kinds. The book says Yoga is the path where you have to come when you realise your main desire is to be limitless. It is a technology for well-being derived from the science of Yoga. There are plenty more in the book in this part but some of the intersting things that everyone should know are in second part. So, i have included the second part summary here. Let's get going...

“As there is a science and technology to create external well-being, there is a whole dimension of science and technology for inner well-being.”
—Sadhguru
